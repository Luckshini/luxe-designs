About Us

Luxe Designs is an interior design firm based in Mauritius, driven by a team of visionary designers who blend style with functionality.

Our philosophy is rooted in creating spaces that reflect the dynamic nature of modern living, emphasizing timeless elegance and adaptive design.

We focus on delivering interiors that balance aesthetic appeal with comfort, ensuring that each space feels uniquely personal yet universally welcoming.

At Luxe Designs, we take pride in our ability to transform environments into beautiful and functional spaces that bring people together.

Our work is grounded in human values, prioritizing the comfort and needs of our clients while infusing each project with a sense of creativity and inspiration.
